1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our content.

2. How We Use Cookies

At SmileyMeaning.com, we use cookies to:

  • Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior via Google Analytics.
  • Show relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Advertising & Google AdSense

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to serve ads on our site.

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To learn more, visit All About Cookies.
