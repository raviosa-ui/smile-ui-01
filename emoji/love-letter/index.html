Real-life usage scenarios

It is widely used when sending long, thoughtful texts to a partner, or when expressing feelings in a long-distance relationship.

People frequently use it on Valentine's Day or anniversaries to accompany a digital gift card or a sweet paragraph of appreciation.

It is also popular in social media captions when thanking followers for an outpouring of support, treating the caption itself as a 'letter' to the fans.

How this emoji affects tone and emotion

Adding this emoji gives a message a sense of thoughtful intention and nostalgic romance.

It makes the text feel significantly more special and intimate, indicating that the words should be read with care.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is a wonderfully sincere way to package a compliment or romantic thought.

In professional settings, it should be strictly avoided. Using an icon of a sealed love letter in corporate communication crosses clear professional boundaries.

When to use

Use this emoji when sending romantic messages, love notes, confessions, or heartfelt written expressions of affection.

When not to use

Avoid using it in professional communication or casual conversations where romantic intent would be inappropriate.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it to deliver purely logistical news or mundane updates can confuse the recipient, as the symbol sets an expectation for emotional depth.

Sending it to a casual acquaintance might be interpreted as an unwanted romantic advance.
