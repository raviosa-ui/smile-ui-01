How this emoji affects tone and emotion

It adds an explosive, highly enthusiastic, and supportive tone to any message.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is perfect for celebrating your friends' life updates.

In professional settings, it is arguably the most acceptable celebration emoji. Managers use it frequently to announce hitting a quarterly goal, and coworkers use it to congratulate each other on promotions.

When to use

Use it to celebrate achievements, good news, birthdays, holidays, or any festive moment.

When not to use

Avoid using it during serious discussions, bad news, or formal complaints.
