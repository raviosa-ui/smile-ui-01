Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is a staple for showing high-energy appreciation for anything from your best friend to your favorite dessert.

When to use

Use this emoji when expressing strong attraction, admiration, excitement, or love toward a person, object, or experience.

When not to use

Avoid using it in formal or professional communication, as it can feel overly emotional, informal, or flirtatious.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it in response to a serious personal struggle or bad news is highly inappropriate, as it radiates overwhelming joy.

Sending this to a stranger online can sometimes come across as overly forward or intensely flirtatious.
