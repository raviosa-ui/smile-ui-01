How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji adds an incredibly laid-back, friendly, and informal tone.

It instantly de-escalates stress, telling the recipient that everything is cool and there are no worries.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is a beloved and highly used friendly response.

In professional settings, it is far too slang-heavy for client interactions, but works great for a quick message to a work friend asking them to hop on a quick call.

When to use

Use this to ask someone to call you, to agree with someone in a relaxed way, or to send positive, 'chill' vibes.

When not to use

Avoid using it in formal, serious, or strict environments where surfer slang or casual gestures feel completely out of place.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it to ask for a highly urgent, emergency phone call might send mixed signals, as the emoji inherently carries a 'relaxed' vibe.
