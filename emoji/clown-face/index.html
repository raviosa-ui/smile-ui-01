Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is a staple of Gen Z and Millennial humor.

In professional settings, it should be entirely avoided.

When to use

Use this when you want to playfully mock yourself for making a bad decision, or to call out a friend's ridiculous logic.

When not to use

Avoid using it in serious professional debates, as calling a coworker or client a 'clown' is highly unprofessional and offensive.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it sincerely to ask if someone wants to go to the circus might confuse them, as they may think you are calling them an idiot.
