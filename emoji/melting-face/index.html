How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji adds a layer of self-deprecating humor and vulnerability.

It signals that the sender is struggling but is still capable of laughing at themselves.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is one of the most relatable emojis for shared venting.

In professional settings, use it only in very relaxed team chats to comment on common stressors like tight deadlines.

When to use

Use this when you are embarrassed, dealing with high heat, or feeling a sense of social dread.

When not to use

Avoid in serious or tragic conversations where a 'melting' metaphor would seem insensitive.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it to describe actual melting objects (like ice cream) is technically a misuse; the emoji is meant to convey human emotion.

Avoid using it to deliver genuine bad news, as the smile makes it look like you aren't taking the tragedy seriously.
