How this emoji affects tone and emotion

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is perfect for showing your friends that you are fully on board with their ideas.

When to use

Use this emoji when expressing excitement, eagerness, warm enthusiasm, and highly positive reactions.

When not to use

Avoid using it in serious, sad, or sensitive discussions where high-energy happiness may feel completely inappropriate.

Overusing it in a single paragraph can make the sender seem a bit overwhelmingly enthusiastic or hyperactive.
