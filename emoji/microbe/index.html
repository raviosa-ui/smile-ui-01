When to use

Use this when warning someone about getting sick, discussing hygiene, studying biology, or jokingly calling out a friend's toxic behavior.

When not to use

Avoid using it repeatedly on someone's social media posts if they are suffering from a serious infection, as it can feel mocking rather than supportive.

Using it to describe food can accidentally imply the food gave you food poisoning, even if you just meant the restaurant looked a bit messy.
