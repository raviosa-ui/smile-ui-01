How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji creates a tone of mystery, loyalty, and strict boundaries.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual text threads, it is a fun, lighthearted way to handle gossip and surprises.

In a corporate environment, it should be avoided. Refusing to communicate visually via a zipper mouth comes off as immature, and implying you are hiding 'company secrets' creates an untrustworthy atmosphere.

When to use

Use this emoji when indicating secrecy, confidentiality, playful silence, or refusing to comment.
