How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji adds a tone of finality, respect, and professionalism.

It signals that a conversation has reached a successful conclusion and both parties are on the same page.

Professional vs casual usage

In professional settings, it is a powerhouse emoji that is almost universally acceptable.

In casual settings, it is used more playfully to 'lock in' plans or to highlight a funny agreement between friends.

When to use

Use this to confirm a plan, finalize a business deal, show mutual respect, or participate in the 'mutual agreement' meme.

When not to use

Avoid using it in highly emotional or romantic conversations where a cold, business-like handshake would feel awkward and disconnected.

Common mistakes and misuse

Using it to respond to a deeply personal or romantic message (like someone saying 'I love you') would be seen as a hilarious but harsh rejection, treating the romance like a business transaction.
