How this emoji affects tone and emotion

When used literally, it is caring and health-conscious.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, the 'chill pill' and 'hard truth' usages are very popular.

In professional settings, it is best reserved for actual discussions about healthcare, pharmaceuticals, or sick leave.

When to use

Use this to remind someone to take their medication, to tell a stressed friend to calm down, or to acknowledge an uncomfortable truth.

When not to use

Avoid using it when discussing serious substance abuse issues, as the cartoonish nature of the emoji can trivialize the topic.

Common mistakes and misuse

Sending this completely out of context can easily make people think you are talking about illegal party drugs.
