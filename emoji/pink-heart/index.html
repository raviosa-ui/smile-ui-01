How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji adds a very sweet, endearing, and highly positive tone to a message.

It makes the sender appear approachable, kind, and emotionally available.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is a top-tier emoji for expressing sweetness to everyone in your life.

In professional settings, it is usually best to stick to the Blue or Yellow heart, unless you work in a highly creative or fashion-focused industry.

When to use

Use this emoji to express gentle love, kindness, appreciation, or emotional warmth.

When not to use

Avoid using it in highly professional or emotionally serious situations where a lighthearted tone would be inappropriate.

Common mistakes and misuse

Because it is very 'cute,' using it to discuss a deeply serious or dark topic can come across as immature or tone-deaf.

Using it too much in a professional setting can make a message seem a bit too casual or informal.
