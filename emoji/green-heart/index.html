How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji adds a refreshing, calm, and highly supportive tone to a message.

Professional vs casual usage

In casual settings, it is perfect for showing your friends and family that you are rooting for their personal growth.

In professional settings, it is a great choice for brands focused on sustainability, health, or organic products to connect with their audience.

When to use

Use this emoji to express caring, supportive, healing, or growth-oriented love, especially in calm or nature-related contexts.

When not to use

Avoid using it when intense passion or dramatic romantic emotion needs to be emphasized.
