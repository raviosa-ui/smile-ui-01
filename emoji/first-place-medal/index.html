How this emoji affects tone and emotion

This emoji carries a highly triumphant, proud, and celebratory tone.

In casual settings, it is a fun way to hype up your friends and celebrate their personal wins.

In professional settings, it is highly appropriate for acknowledging a top-performing salesperson, a team that won a corporate award, or an employee who exceeded their quarterly goals.

When to use
